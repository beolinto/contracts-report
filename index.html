Key insight

The median signing time is very low (0-5 hours), but averages reach up to 48.6 hours.

Most merchants sign quickly during onboarding, but a tail of slow signers pulls up the average. The week of 23 Mar saw the biggest spike (48.6h avg) - coinciding with the full Inside Sales rollout on 16 Mar, suggesting new agents may not be guiding merchants through the app login. The declining sign rate (95.7% → 73.9%) confirms this as a scaling issue.

Key insights

Contract merchants activate 6.2pp lower overall but activate faster (2.0 vs 3.0 median days). ISA channel leads with 59.1% and fastest time (2.9 days).

The lower activation rate is dragged down by 164 unsigned merchants. The cohort is only 5 weeks old. Weekly trends show contract and no-contract rates track closely, with both declining for recent weeks (cohort maturity effect, not a contract-specific issue).

Non-contract merchants have 2x higher avg TPV (€9,018 vs €4,543) - but this is a pool size and age effect.

The non-contract pool is 3.9x larger (1,463 vs 372 activated) and includes merchants from all channels and onboarding periods. Contract merchants are newer (all since 10 Mar). TPV comparison will become meaningful as the contract cohort matures.

Conclusions

Contract adoption is strong - 675 contracts in 5 weeks

23.1% of all GB onboarded customers received a contract. Weekly creation volumes jumped from 23 to 198 after the full Inside Sales rollout. The signing rate is 75.7% overall.

Most merchants sign fast, but a tail creates risk

39.3% sign within 1 hour and 70.6% within 24 hours. However, 164 haven't signed at all - 27 of whom are already transacting without a contract.

Signed merchants activate faster

Despite a 6.2pp lower overall activation rate, contract merchants reach first transaction 1 day faster (median 2.0 vs 3.0 days). The gap is expected to narrow as unsigned cases are resolved.

The signing rate is declining week over week

From 95.7% to 73.9% as contracts scale to more agents. This is a process gap (agents not guiding app login), not a product gap.

Biggest risk: 76 unsigned merchants stuck in CDD Manual Review

Combined with 73 "likely lost" merchants, coordinated outreach between sales and compliance is needed.
